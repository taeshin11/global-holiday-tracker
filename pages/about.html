About Global Holiday Tracker

Global Holiday Tracker is a free calendar and countdown dashboard that displays upcoming public holidays and commercial events by country. It is designed for global marketers, e-commerce sellers, HR managers, and anyone who needs to plan around international holidays.

What We Offer

  • Multi-Country View: Track holidays across up to 6 countries simultaneously with color-coded visualization.
  • Countdown Timers: Live countdown to upcoming holidays and major e-commerce events.
  • Monthly Calendar: Visual calendar with holiday markers for easy planning.
  • E-commerce Events: Key shopping events like Black Friday, Singles Day, Amazon Prime Day, and more.
  • .ics Export: Download holidays to your calendar (Google Calendar, Apple Calendar, Outlook).
  • 8 Languages: Available in English, Korean, Japanese, Chinese, Spanish, German, French, and Portuguese.

Data Sources

Public holiday data is provided by the Nager.Date API, a free and open public holiday API supporting over 100 countries. E-commerce event dates are maintained manually and are approximate - actual dates may vary by retailer and region.

Who Is This For?

  • E-commerce Sellers: Plan inventory, promotions, and shipping around global holidays.
  • Digital Marketers: Schedule international campaigns aligned with holidays and shopping events.
  • HR Managers: Track holidays for distributed teams across multiple countries.
  • Travelers: Plan trips around public holidays to maximize experiences or avoid closures.
  • Event Planners: Schedule events and content around global holidays.

Cost

Global Holiday Tracker is completely free to use. No registration, no API keys, no hidden costs.
