Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Global Holiday Tracker is a client-side web application. We collect minimal data:

  • Local Storage Data: Your country selections, language preference, and visitor count are stored locally in your browser's localStorage and sessionStorage. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly share it.
  • Usage Analytics: We may log anonymous usage data (page views, feature usage) via Google Sheets webhook for service improvement. This data does not include personally identifiable information.
  • Ad Networks: Third-party ad providers (Google AdSense, Adsterra) may collect data as described in their respective privacy policies.

2. How We Use Information

  • To remember your country selections and language preferences between visits.
  • To provide accurate holiday data via the Nager.Date API.
  • To improve the service through anonymous usage analytics.
  • To display relevant advertisements.

3. Data Storage

All user preferences are stored locally in your browser. We do not maintain user accounts or databases. API response data is cached in sessionStorage with a 1-hour expiration for performance.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Nager.Date API: Public holiday data provider (date.nager.at)
  • Google AdSense: Advertising (Google Privacy Policy)
  • Adsterra: Advertising (Adsterra Privacy Policy)
  • Vercel: Hosting provider (Vercel Privacy Policy)
  • Google Fonts: Font delivery (Google Privacy Policy)

5. Cookies

Global Holiday Tracker itself does not use cookies. However, third-party services (ad networks, font providers) may set cookies as part of their operation.

6. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
